In this job, you will be presented with social media comments and a respective label associated with each one of them, stating whether the comment is appropriate or not to be on the platform. Please, for each comment, review its label and provide a justification for your judgment.

  • Appropriate means that the comment should be allowed in social media platforms such as facebook and twitter.
  • Inappropriate means that the comment should not be allowed be on a social media platform, where it can be seen by everyone.

Steps

  • 1) First, read the comment.
  • 2) Next, read the label.
  • 3) Then, determine whether the label is correct or not.
  • 4) Finally, provide a justification for your answer.
