Consent Form

You are being invited to participate in a research study in the content moderation research field.

Why are we doing this research study?

This study is being done by students from Delft University of Technology and its purpose is to understand how content moderation tasks in social media platforms can become more accurate, improving the internet and especially social media websites and benefiting everyone who uses them.

Who can participate in this research study?

This study targets people of all gender and ages residing in the United States of America.

What will I be asked to do and how much time will it take?

If you agree to take part in this study, you will be asked to moderate 5 short social media comments. Specifically, you’ll be given a label for each comment, stating whether it is appropriate or not, and you’ll have to review the label, determining its correctness and provide justifications for your judgement. The estimate time of completion of this task is 5 minutes

What are my risks of being in this research study?

We believe there are minimal risks associated with this study, however some of the comments might show sensitive and inappropriate language, possibly featuring hate speech, racism, obscenity, etc.

How will my personal information be protected?

To the best of our ability your answers in this study will remain confidential.

What happens if I say yes, but I change my mind later?

You do not have to be in this study if you do not want to. If you agree to be in the study, but later change your mind, you may drop out at any time.
